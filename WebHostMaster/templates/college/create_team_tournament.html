Tournament Structure

Team Tournament Format:
  • Up to 4 teams with 5 members each
  • Includes events: Football, Dodgeball, and Debate
  • Points are awarded based on rankings in each event
  • Overall tournament rankings track team performances
Events:
  • Football: 5v5 football matches with standard rules
  • Dodgeball: Team-based elimination format
  • Debate: Team debates on assigned topics with judging
Note: These events will be automatically created for this tournament.

After Creation

After creating the tournament, you'll need to:

  1. Register teams (5 members each)
  2. Schedule the events
  3. Record results
  4. Track overall tournament rankings
