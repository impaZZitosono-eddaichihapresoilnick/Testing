Math Quiz Tournament Rules

Quiz Format

  • Each round consists of 10 questions
  • Time limit of 60 seconds per question
  • Each match consists of 3 rounds

Scoring System

  • Correct Answer: 10 points
  • Wrong Answer: 0 points
  • Highest total score across all rounds wins
